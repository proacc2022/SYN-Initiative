HISTORY -

So, the history behind the cration of SYN Intiative is that once all the three creators of this page were seating together and they thought of participating in a Marathon.So,they ran the marathon but they were disappointed due to the fact that the people were throwing water bottles and plastic pouches everywhere. So,they thought of why not to organize a marathon to create awareness about the environment and the money collected by the marathon can be used to plant more trees.This is how all of it started and We are excited to tell you that on 3rd December 2020 we will be completing our 10 years.We are grateful that 10,000 people are connected to our Intiative.

PURPOSE -

The purpose behind the Intiative is promotion of environmental awareness.While the Intiative is a cause within itself, it also funds smaller causes and corporate social responsibility projects to help create a better future.We are also starting Plantation drives from our 10th Anniversary as the benefits these trees will have on the world around us are vital for greener living, protecting natural habitats, providing cleaner air, improving natural water supplies, fighting the effects of global warming and more!We are also having Marathons that are about cherishing the spirit of environmental sustainability and securing a green future for our progeny. This marathon is in line to be one of the most unique, exciting and entertaining runs. It is a perfect amalgamation of active healthy living for today to create a better tomorrow. The principal objective of this marathon is to raise awareness about the worsening discrepancy between nature and human life.
