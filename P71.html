THIS AGREEMENT is made and entered into as of this [date] (the “Effective Date”) by and between SYN, a Delaware corporation with offices at 1234 Peter Road, Mumbai, India (“SYN”) and OUT-OF-SITE WEB SITE DEVELOPMENT SYN.


W I T N E S S E T H


WHEREAS, SYN desires to engage DEVELOPER to develop, create, test, and deliver a Web Site to be known as “SYN Inititative” as a work made for hire and to house the Web Site on DEVELOPER’s Web Server and make the Web Site available for browsing on the Internet; and
WHEREAS, DEVELOPER is interested in undertaking such work; and
WHEREAS, SYN and DEVELOPER mutually desire to set forth the terms applicable to such work;
NOW, THEREFORE, for the mutual consideration set forth herein, the adequacy of which is hereby acknowledged, SYN and DEVELOPER, intending to be legally bound, hereby agree as follows:

1. Developer Responsibilities



A. Scope of Work
SYN hereby retains the services of DEVELOPER to design, develop and host a Web Site and Intranet (collectively the “Web Site”) for SYN in accordance with the proposal submitted by DEVELOPER to SYN dated [date] (the “Proposal”), a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit A and the terms of which are expressly incorporated herein by reference.

B. Schedule
The “Schedule” for the development of SYN Web Site and Intranet is attached hereto as Exhibit B.

C. Changes
Changes to this Agreement or to any of the specifications of the Web Site or Intranet in any of the specifications thereof shall become effective only when a written change request is executed by the Executive Director of SYN and DEVELOPER. DEVELOPER agrees to notify SYN promptly of any factor, occurrence, or event coming to its attention that may affect DEVELOPER’s ability to meet the requirements of this Agreement, or that is likely to occasion any material delay in the Schedule.

D. Demonstration at Trade Show
DEVELOPER agrees to exhibit and demonstrate the Web Site during the Trade Show to be held in India on [date]. SYN shall provide DEVELOPER with appropriate space for such demonstration. DEVELOPER shall provide all necessary computers and personnel to effect such demonstration.

2. Web Site Design



A. Design
The design of SYN’s Web Site shall be in substantial conformity with the material provided to DEVELOPER by SYN. DEVELOPER shall develop SYN’s Web Site to project the highest professional image. DEVELOPER shall not include any of the following in the Web Site or in SYN’s directory on DEVELOPER’s Web Server: text, graphics, sound, or animations that might be viewed as offensive or related in any way to sex or any illegal activities; links to other sites that might be viewed as offensive or related in any way to sex or any illegal activities; impressionistic or cartoon-like graphics (unless provided by SYN); invisible text, text that is present only when a “webcrawler” or other web indexing tool accesses the Web Site, or any other type of hidden text, hidden information, hidden graphics, or other hidden materials; or destructive elements or destructive programming of any type.

B. Materials Provided by SYN
All materials to be supplied by SYN may be provided on floppy disks, 100 megabyte ZIP cartridges or via File Transfer Protocol (“FTP”). Files will be provided in HTML format, standard word processing Text format or, if images, as TIFF’s GIFF’S, JPEG’s or Photoshop files.

C. Specifications for Home Page
SYN’s Web Site will consist of a Home Page (the “first” page for the Web Site) that can be reached by typing one of the following Uniform Resource Locators (“URLs”) into a Web Browser. DEVELOPER will use its best efforts to register the “SYN.ORG” domain name for the benefit of SYN (or such other name as may be registrable and acceptable to SYN) and will assign all rights thereto to SYN. SYN agrees to pay all registration fees associated with such registration. In addition to the foregoing, a database will be included of members in the licensing industry.

D. Accessibility Of Web Site During Construction
Throughout the construction of the prototype and the final Web Site, the Web Site shall be accessible to SYN. Until SYN has approved the final Web Site, none of the Web Pages for SYN’s Web Site will be accessible to end users unless the end users have entered the correct user id and password.

E. Project Planning Meetings
After both parties have signed this Agreement, the parties shall meet at SYN or a mutually convenient location and at a mutually convenient date and time to discuss project planning. The parties shall endeavor to hold this meeting in one week after both parties have signed this Agreement.

F. Submission To Index Sites
At the time that DEVELOPER conducts the first “walk through” of SYN’s final Web Site, DEVELOPER will register SYN’s URL with the following search engines: Yahoo, Infoseek, Alta Vista, Web Crawler and Excite!. DEVELOPER will supply a list of other sites (up to 50 sites) with which SYN may also choose to register.

G. Delivery of Deliverables
Upon SYN’s approval of its final Web Site, or upon termination of this Agreement, whichever occurs earlier, DEVELOPER shall deliver to SYN all Code, Documentation, reports and other materials developed by DEVELOPER in the course of its performance under this Agreement and any other items reasonably necessary for the operation of SYN’s Wet) Site (other than third party operating system software, third party networking software, Web Browsers and hardware) and all changes and enhancements thereto (the “Deliverables”). Documentation shall be delivered in printed format and in electronic format. Code shall be delivered in electronic format. The transfer of electronic materials shall be accomplished by copying them to floppy disks, 100 megabyte ZIP cartridges or via File Transfer Protocol (“FTP”). Files will be provided in HTML format, standard word processing Text format or, if images, as TIFF’s GIFF’S, JPEG’s or Photoshop files. DEVELOPER shall maintain its back-ups and one set of the final materials provided to SYN for a period of six months after SYN’s approval of its final Web Site. If this Agreement is terminated prior to final approval, or at the expiration of this six month period, DEVELOPER will destroy all of its copies of SYN’s Web Site (including all back-ups thereof) and “wipe” all files constituting final or working copies of SYN’s Web Site (other than the final copy hosted on DEVELOPER’s Web Server and one backup copy thereof) from DEVELOPER’s computers and back-up materials unless otherwise directed in writing by SYN.

H. Advertising Transaction Fees
DEVELOPER agrees to assist SYN in the sale of any advertising and/or database searches or other programs to generate revenues from the use of the Web Site by third parties. In this regard, DEVELOPER will provide assistance in developing such programs for SYN. In such event, the parties agree to enter into good faith negotiations to reasonably compensate DEVELOPER for such services.

3. Web Site Hosting



A. Server Hosting
DEVELOPER agrees, at SYN’s option, to maintain SYN’s Web Site on DEVELOPER’s Web Server on a month to month basis, and to make maintenance modifications to SYN’s Web Site from time to time in accordance with SYN’s directions. Such modifications shall be implemented within five (5) business days of DEVELOPER’s receipt of SYN’s changes if the changes are easily implemented, and within ten (10) business days of DEVELOPER’s receipt of SYN’s changes if the changes are not easily implemented. As part of this service, DEVELOPER agrees to make SYN’s Web Site available to Internet users approximately 24 hours per day, to back-up SYN’s Web Site at least once every two weeks, and to store said back-up materials in a safe and secure environment, fit for the back-up media, and not located at the same location as DEVELOPER’s Web Server. Also as part of this service, DEVELOPER agrees to use its best efforts to ensure reasonable response times for users accessing SYN’s Web Site.

B. Back-Up Copies
Upon notice from SYN not more often than once each month, and also in the event of SYN’s termination of its use of DEVELOPER’s Web Server as the host for SYN’s Web Site, DEVELOPER agrees to transfer a complete copy of SYN’s then-current Web Site, including all Code therefor, to SYN, said transfer to occur by either copying them to floppy disks, 100 megabyte ZIP cartridges or via File Transfer Protocol (“FFP”). Files will be provided in HTML format, standard word processing Text format or, if images, as TIFF’s GIFF’S, JPEG’s or Photoshop files. The transfer method will be selected by SYN in its discretion no later than 24 hours before the time the transfer is to take place. in the event such transfer results from SYN’s termination of its use of DEVELOPER’s Web Server as the host for SYN’s Web Site, DEVELOPER shall maintain one complete electronic version of SYN’s Web Site, including all Code therefor (and shall “ wipe” all other versions thereof off of its computers and media, including back-up copies), until SYN informs DEVELOPER in writing that the transferred files appear to be complete, at which time DEVELOPER shall “wipe” its final copy of SYN’s Web Site off of its computers and media.

C. Transaction Logging
During the time that SYN’s Web Site is located on DEVELOPER’s Web Server, DEVELOPER will make available on a monthly basis and free of charge an analysis of SYN Web Site traffic, including source IP address, most commonly viewed pages and any other such data reasonably requested by SYN. DEVELOPER shall set aside a portion of its server, such portion only accessible by designated SYN staff or members, in which such analysis resides. The analysis may be viewed or printed out by SYN at its option.

4. Compensation



A. Price for Web Site Creation
The total price for all of the work set forth in the Agreement (excluding the Server Hosting and excluding post-approval modifications not implemented by SYN) shall be SEVEN THOUSAND, FIVE HUNDRED UNITED STATES DOLLARS ($7,500.00) (the “Development Fee”). This price covers all work of whatever nature on SYN’s Web Site contemplated in this Agreement (excluding Server Hosting and post-approval modifications not implemented by SYN). When both parties have signed this AGREEMENT, SYN will forward to DEVELOPER SEVEN THOUSAND, FIVE, HUNDRED UNITED STATES DOLLARS ($7,500.00) and an additional TWO THOUSAND, FIVE HUNDRED UNITED STATES DOLLARS ($2,500.00) when the Web Site is operational in a form reasonably acceptable to SYN.

B. Price for Web Site Hosting
The price for the Server Hosting shall be ONE THOUSAND UNITED STATES DOLLARS ($1,000.00) per month (the “Hosting Fee”). Charges for post- approval modifications to SYN’s Web Site or changes or additions to the material on the Web Site (including the data base) shall be free if submitted to DEVELOPER by SYN as “ready to implement” HTML pages. The cost of Server Hosting shall not increase for a period of one year from the date of SYN’s acceptance of its final Web Site. The Hosting Fee shall be commence on the date the final Web Site is fully operational and accepted by SYN and future Hosting Fees shall be due and payable on subsequent monthly anniversary dates of such operational date.

C. Invoicing
Thereafter, DEVELOPER shall invoice SYN on a bi-weekly basis for the amount of work done during the applicable two-week period. All payments are due fifteen (15) days after receipt of a properly payable invoice. If there is a dispute with regard to whether work was actually completed or whether an invoice is properly payable, the amount of the invoice in dispute shall not be due until the dispute is resolved.

D. Expenses
The prices set forth above are inclusive of expenses. Except as expressly agreed otherwise in writing by SYN, DEVELOPER shall bear all of its own expenses arising from its performance of its obligations under this Agreement, including (without limitation) expenses for facilities, work spaces, utilities, management, clerical and reproduction services, supplies, and the like. SYN shall have no obligation to provide office space, work facilities, equipment, clerical services, programming services, or the like.

E. Links
DEVELOPER may, with the prior express written approval of SYN, provide a link from the members-only section of SYN Web Site to a Marketplace area designated by DEVELOPER and acceptable to SYN. The purpose of the Marketplace area is to sell products or generate other on-line transactions. Revenues generated from SYN members in this area will be divided between DEVELOPER and SYN as mutually agreed to between the parties.

5. Confidentiality



A. Confidentiality
DEVELOPER shall treat this project as confidential. After SYN has approved its final Web Site, however, DEVELOPER may list SYN as a client of DEVELOPER and may include a link to SYN’s Web Site on DEVELOPER’s Web Site. DEVELOPER represents and warrants that any information provided to SYN in the course of entering into this Agreement or performing any work hereunder shall not be confidential or proprietary to DEVELOPER.

C. Confidential Information of SYN
From time to time SYN may provide its own confidential business and technical information to DEVELOPER in connection with the work to be performed by DEVELOPER hereunder. Such information shall be designated as confidential upon or prior to disclosure by SYN. In addition, the preparation and specifications of the Deliverables shall in all instances be treated as confidential, unless and until disclosed publicly by SYN.
